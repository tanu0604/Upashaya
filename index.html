About Us

The objective of this project is to develop an advanced Health Monitoring System that utilizes blockchain technology for secure data management and an AI chatbot for personalized healthcare interactions. By combining these technologies, the system aims to enhance data privacy, security, and patient engagement while providing real-time health monitoring and support.

  • AI Chatbot

  • User Interface Design
